Back To The Wall

  • High Concept: Make it to the end of the level without falling of being eaten by the wall of flesh.
  • Player Experience Goals: Player should feel concerned whether or not they will make it to the end without being eaten while also "on-edge"
  • Genre: Sidescrolling Chase game
  • Game play: The game is simple, press left and right arrow to move in those directions and press the spacebar to jump. Jump over gaps and up terrain in order to make it to the end of the level.
  • Prototype goal: Make it to the end of the level alive in order to kill the wall of flesh
  • Features: Right-Left arrow keys to move left and right. Space bar to jump. The player only gets one life
  • Setting/Premise: In the middle of nowhere.
  • Story: The player has made it to the pits of hell where the wall of flesh resides. The only way to escape is to keep moving to the right.
  • Target Audience: Anyone who enjoys platformers, especially mario.
  • Play time: under 10 minutes.
  • Strategies: Keep moving to the right as much as you can. Stopping can let the wall of flesh catch up to you.
